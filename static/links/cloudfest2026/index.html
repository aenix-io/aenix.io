The Platform

Sovereign Cloud-in-a-Box

At its core, Cozystack is designed to help organizations launch their own cloud infrastructure quickly and reliably. We describe the concept as "Sovereign Cloud-in-a-Box."

Managed Kubernetes

Simple, full-featured and pre-configured Kubernetes that just works. Like in AWS, but on your bare-metal servers.

Virtual Machines

Robust virtualization system for running Windows, Linux and other OS with ready images.

Databases as a Service

DBaaS for PostgreSQL, MySQL, Redis, Mongo, ClickHouse — provisioned on demand with backups, HA and ultimate bare-metal performance.

Object Storage (S3)

S3-compatible object storage. Store and serve data without external dependencies.

GPU as a Service

First-class GPU support. AI/ML training and inference pipelines at scale are deployed to virtual machines and Kubernetes pods.

Managed Apps

Kafka, RabbitMQ, load balancers, HTTP caching services and the ability to easily add any other apps you need.

All running on bare-metal servers and managed from a unified platform. In most cases, a new cloud environment can be deployed in 1–2 weeks.

Platform Editions

Who Cozystack Is Built For

Cozystack is used by several different types of organizations. At CloudFest, we will be presenting different platform editions tailored for specific infrastructure scenarios.

ISP Edition

Hosting & MSP

Designed for hosting companies, MSPs, and data centers looking for a modern alternative to OpenStack. Launch new cloud services quickly while maintaining full control over your infrastructure.

For: Data centers, hosting companies, MSPs
Public Cloud Edition

Multi-Tenant Cloud

A multi-hypervisor cloud control plane with users, projects, and quotas, plus a cloud-like user experience and multi-region options. Integrates with third-party infrastructure and service providers.

For: Large data centres, mature cloud providers
Enterprise Edition

Sovereign Enterprise

A private and hybrid sovereign cloud — secure, automated, and easy to extend. Built for multi-DC setups, compliance, and enterprise integrations. Connects to VMware, OpenNebula, OpenShift, and more.

For: Banks, government, regulated industries
IDP Edition

Developer Platform

An Internal Developer Platform layer on top: GitLab automation, Argo CD workflows, and APIs — so your engineers spend less time on routine work.

For: Platform engineering teams
AI/ML Edition

AI Infrastructure

AI platform automation out of the box: standard AI workloads, service APIs, and ready-to-use blueprints, DBs, apps and models — from idea to running jobs faster.

For: AI/ML teams, research labs
Pure Cozystack

Self-Hosted Cloud

The cloud experience on your own or leased servers (Hetzner, etc). Reduce AWS-level bills by 2–7x without giving up reliability or convenience. Go live and migrate from public clouds in 1–2 weeks.

For: Cost-conscious teams, startups, product teams
