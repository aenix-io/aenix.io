## What's covered

- Cloud architecture review and target design (public, private, hybrid)
- Infrastructure-as-code maturity (Terraform, OpenTofu, Pulumi, Crossplane)
- Observability stack engineering (VictoriaMetrics, Grafana, OpenTelemetry)
- CI/CD and GitOps maturity
- Container orchestration (Kubernetes platform decisions)
- Network and identity engineering
- FinOps integration
- Compliance posture (DORA, NIS2, sectoral)
