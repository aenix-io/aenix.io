## Was bewertet wird (8 Dimensionen)

1. **Workload-Portabilität** — IaC-Coverage, Drift-Detection, Environment-Parität
2. **GitOps-Adoption** — deklarative Ops, PR-driven Changes, Audit-Trail
3. **Observability-Vereinheitlichung** — Single Pane of Glass, SLO-Disziplin, Alert-Hygiene
4. **Secrets-Handhabung** — zentrales Management, Rotation, Audit
5. **Identity-Modell** — Workforce ↔ Workload Identity-Federation
6. **Multi-Tenancy** — Namespace-, Project- oder Tenant-CRD-Modell
7. **Disaster-Recovery-Posture** — Backup, Restore, DR-getestete Kadenz
8. **Self-Service-Tiefe** — Golden Paths, Time-to-Environment

Für jede Dimension 5-Stufen-Rubrik (Pre-platform → Mature platform engineering).
