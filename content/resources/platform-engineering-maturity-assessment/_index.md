## What's assessed (8 dimensions)

1. **Workload portability** — IaC coverage, drift detection, environment parity
2. **GitOps adoption** — declarative ops, PR-driven changes, audit trail
3. **Observability unification** — single pane of glass, SLO discipline, alert hygiene
4. **Secrets handling** — central management, rotation, audit
5. **Identity model** — workforce ↔ workload identity federation
6. **Multi-tenancy** — namespace, project, or tenant CRD model
7. **Disaster-recovery posture** — backup, restore, DR-tested cadence
8. **Self-service depth** — golden paths, time-to-environment

For each dimension, 5-stage rubric (Pre-platform → Mature platform engineering).
