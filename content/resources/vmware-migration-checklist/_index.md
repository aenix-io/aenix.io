## What's in the checklist

- **Inventory** — workload count, OS mix, criticality tiers
- **Dependencies** — vSAN, NSX, vCD, vRealize, custom integrations
- **Network and storage redesign** — what maps directly, what needs new architecture
- **Multi-tenancy model** — vCD organizations to Cozystack Tenant CRD
- **AI/GPU workloads** — VMware vGPU vs KubeVirt vGPU
- **Sovereignty and compliance** — DORA/NIS2 alignment, key custody
- **Operational readiness** — runbooks, on-call, knowledge transfer
- **Cost trajectory** — TCO inputs, commitment expirations, repatriation candidates
