## Aenix Releases Ænix Platform 1.0 — a Stable Enterprise Cloud Platform Based on Cozystack

Aenix has announced the release of Ænix Platform 1.0 — a production-ready cloud platform for building private and public clouds.

Ænix Platform 1.0 is based on Cozystack 0.38.4, an open-source cloud platform and CNCF Sandbox project, developed and maintained by Aenix together with the open-source community. The platform also incorporates Talos Linux, Aenix's proprietary billing modules, and comprehensive operational documentation.

The 1.0 release marks an important milestone, confirming that Ænix Platform has reached a stable, production-grade state. It is already widely used by Aenix customers in real-world production environments and has proven itself as a modern and user-friendly solution for building private and public cloud infrastructures.

## Key Features

1. Installation and operational documentation for air-gapped and offline environments
2. White labeling capabilities, allowing customers to fully brand the platform with their own logos and identity
3. Built-in components required for commercial cloud operations, including billing and resource usage accounting

Ænix Platform 1.0 is designed for organizations seeking to build and operate their own Kubernetes-based cloud platforms while maintaining full control over infrastructure, data, and business models.
